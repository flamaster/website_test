This Privacy Policy explains how Piotr Lupinski ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use the "Playground FL2" mobile application ("the App").
The "Playground FL2" App is designed to be a simple visual experience. It displays a curated list of images that are bundled within the app itself and presented in a random order.

1. Information We CollectThe "Playground FL2" App is designed to be as privacy-friendly as possible. Therefore:
• We do not collect any personal information from you. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, phone number, location data, usage patterns, or any other identifiable information.
• We do not track your activity within the app. The app simply displays images from a predefined list.
• We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.
• The app does not use any analytics tools, crash reporting tools, or advertising SDKs.

2. How We Use Your InformationSince we do not collect any of your information, there is no information to use. The App functions solely on your device to display the images.

3. Disclosure of Your InformationAs we do not collect any personal information, there is no information to disclose to any third parties.

4. Security of Your DataAs no data is collected or stored by the App, there are no security concerns related to data handling within the App itself.

5. Your Privacy RightsBecause the App does not collect any personal information, you do not have specific privacy rights to exercise concerning data collected by this App.

6. Children's PrivacyThe App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy PolicyWe may update this Privacy Policy in the future if any changes are made to how the App functions regarding data handling (which is currently none).
