Playground FL2 — Privacy Policy

By Piotr Lupinski — Last updated: October 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Piotr Lupinski ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use the "Playground FL2" mobile application (the "App"). The "Playground FL2" App is designed to be a simple visual experience. It displays a curated list of images that are bundled within the app itself and presented in a random order.

1. Information We Collect

The "Playground FL2" App is designed to be as privacy-friendly as possible. Therefore:

  • We do not collect any personal information from you. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, phone number, location data, usage patterns, or any other identifiable information.
  • We do not track your activity within the app. The app simply displays images from a predefined list bundled in the app package.
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.
  • The app does not use any analytics tools, crash reporting tools, or advertising SDKs.

2. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any of your information, there is no information to use. The App functions solely on your device to display the images.

3. Disclosure of Your Information

As we do not collect any personal information, there is no information to disclose to any third parties.

4. Security of Your Data

As no data is collected or stored by the App, there are no security concerns related to data handling within the App itself.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Because the App does not collect any personal information, you do not have specific privacy rights to exercise concerning data collected by this App.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy in the future if any changes are made to how the App functions regarding data handling (which is currently none). We will post any new policy updates here and update the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us:

  • By email: studio@flamaster2.com
  • Via our website: flamaster2.com
Key aspects of this policy: No data collection, no third-party tools, images bundled in the app.
